Protocol
=======

### Reagents


| Name          | CAS-Number | Amount |
|---------------|------------|--------|
| D-Mannitol    | 69-65-8    | 20 G/L | 
| Soybean flour | N/A        | 20 G/L |
| Agar Powder   | 9002-18-0  | 20 G/L |


### Steps

- Mix components with TAP (!) water
- Autoclave TWICE (!) using a program with 115 °C for 15 mins.
- Add 1 mL/L [Trace Element Solution](trace-element-solution-260720.md)

### Notes

- Single autoclaving often leads to residual contamination. This is likely due to the soy flour forming aggregates that protect the spores.
