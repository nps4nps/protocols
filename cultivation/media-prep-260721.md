# Protocol

## Overview

Preparation of microbial cultivation media

## Prerequisites

### Reagents

- 1M HCl
- 1M NaOH

## Steps

- Weight in components into a 1L beaker
- Add ~90% of the final volume of water to the beaker
- IF pH needs to be adjusted, stir medium with magnet stirrer while measuring the pH. Adjust with 1M HCl and/or 1M NaOH
- Add water to final volume using a graded cylinder
- Transfer medium to schott-bottle, label, add autoclaving tape
- Autoclave with slights loose cap. The standard process is 20 mins at 121 °C.

## Outcomes

Autoclaved media.

## Notes

- [Trace element solutions](../cultivation_media/trace-element-solution-260720.md) must be added after autoclaving
- Specific media have special instructions
