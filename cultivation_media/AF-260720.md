Protocol
=======

### Reagents


| Name          | CAS-Number | Amount |
|---------------|------------|--------|
| Dextrose      | 50-99-7    | 20 G/L | 
| Soybean flour | N/A        | 8 G/L  |
| Yeast extract | 8013-01-02  | 2 G/L  |
| NaCl          | 7647-14-5  | 1 G/L  |
| CaCO3         | 207-439-9  | 4 G/L  |

### Steps

- Mix components with demineralized water
- Adjust to pH 7.3 using NaOH 1M
- Autoclave TWICE (!) using a program with 115 °C for 15 mins.

### Notes

- Single autoclaving often leads to residual contamination. This is likely due to the soy flour forming aggregates that protect the spores.
